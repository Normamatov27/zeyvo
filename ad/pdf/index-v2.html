VI · INTELLIGENCEML LAYER
— 09 —
AI
The intelligence layer

Not "AI features". Operational mathematics.

A queue is a stochastic process. We model it like one. Service-duration distributions per skill × counter × time-of-day feed a live predictor that updates every second — and a forecasting model that warns of overload 30 minutes before it lands.

01
PREDICTIVE ETA

Per-customer, per-second

Bayesian update of expected service duration as each ticket clears. The model improves with every visit. Cold-start handled with industry priors.

P50 error±90 sec
P95 error±4 min
02
OVERLOAD FORECAST

30-min advance warning

A surge predictor reading the rate of ticket creation against historical peak curves. Managers get a 30-min heads-up to call in reinforcements or pre-route customers.

Lead time30 min
Precision0.84
03
CROSS-BRANCH BALANCE

The network as one organism

When Branch A overloads, customers joining for the same service are offered Branch B (with predicted wait + travel time displayed). The network self-balances.

Wait reduction−34%
Acceptance rate61%
04
CAPACITY PLANNING

Quarter-ahead forecast

Long-horizon throughput forecasting drives staffing recommendations: "Branch X needs +2 tellers on Mondays Q3." Decision-grade, not dashboard art.

Horizon90 days
